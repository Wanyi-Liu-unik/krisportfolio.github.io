R - Sentiment Analysis for Stock Prediction

This study investigates the relationship between Tesla-related news headlines and TSLA stock prices over the past year. It aims to address key research questions:

  • Can sentiment analysis of news headlines be used as a predictive indicator for short-term stock price movements?
  • How does sentiment in Tesla-related news headlines correlate with stock price and stock price movement?
  • What is the best way to predict short-term stock price?

Using sentiment analysis techniques and machine learning models, the project explores correlations and prediction accuracy, providing insights into effective strategies for stock price prediction.
